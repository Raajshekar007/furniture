Our Story

Furniture Built
to Last Lifetimes

FORMA was born from a simple belief: that the spaces we live in shape the people we become. Founded in 2012 by a team of furniture craftspeople and designers, we combine traditional joinery with contemporary form.

Every piece is made in our Bengaluru workshop using sustainably sourced hardwoods and natural finishes. We believe furniture should improve with age — becoming part of your story, not just your home.
